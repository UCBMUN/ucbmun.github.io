Tik Tok: The Hype House

Background Guide coming soon.

Contact: hype@ucbmun.org



With the rise of social media, Tik Tok has quickly become one of the most popular platforms for individuals around the world to connect and interact. Through the COVID–19 pandemic, many of us found solace in joining worldwide trends––whether they were Tik Tok dances, dalgona coffee, or looking up to rising creators. Since March 2020, some of these creators have joined content houses in an effort to rise even higher up the A list, each member garnering tens of millions of followers. With the recent release of their reality TV show, The Hype House, these creators have met with backlash from viewers claiming that Tik Tokers aren't A–list celebrities worthy of a reality show. Season 2, however, is on the rise and producers are looking to cut the cast, giving airtime only to influencers with good publicity. In this crisis, delegates will dive into the glamour and ugliness of Los Angeles and cancel culture to compete for the limited spots of Season 2 casting––who knows what you'll find out?


Kaavya Thanigaivelan

Chair

Welcome to the Hype House!

My name is Kaavya Thanigaivelan, and I am excited be your Chair for Tik Tok: The Hype House! In this new age of media, Tik Tok has become a major platform catering to curious minds, young and old, around the world. Especially as consumers of this content, we seldom consider the inner workings of how certain content came to be or the influential powers behind creators' platforms. In this committee, you all will have the chance to step up to the role of popular creators and experience the competition and complexities of being an influencer!
