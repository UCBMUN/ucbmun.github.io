About the Committee

Step into the dazzling world of music, politics, and cultural diplomacy with the Eurovision Song Contest Committee. Representing nations from across Europe (and beyond), delegates will not only craft and promote their country’s entry but also navigate the complex web of alliances, rivalries, and public opinion that define Eurovision. Behind the glitter and spectacle lies the high-stakes strategy of bloc voting, media influence, and soft power projection. Will your delegation secure the coveted victory through artistry, persuasion, or shrewd political maneuvering? Join us for a committee where music meets international relations, and every vote counts. We can’t wait to see you all on stage in Vienna this spring!
