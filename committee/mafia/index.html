About the Committee

Buongiorno Delegates!

As dawn breaks over the Sicilian hills, the shadows of Palermo stir with whispers of power, betrayal, and ambition. You, the esteemed members of Cosa Nostra’s inner circle, are here to navigate the treacherous waters of crime, loyalty, and influence. The year is 1865, a time of shifting power within the mafia. In this world, every glance holds a secret, every handshake seals a deal, and every smile hides a dagger. Your decisions will shape the future of the mafia empire, and nothing is as it seems. Expect a journey filled with clandestine meetings, high-stakes negotiations, and unexpected twists.

Just when you think you’ve secured your position, an unforeseen turn will challenge your wit and resolve. In the shadows, power struggles brew, and reputations are always at stake. The law enforcement’s grip tightens, and amidst this chaos, only the cunning will survive. Every move must be meticulously planned, every word carefully chosen. The consequences are as unpredictable as they are severe. Let us embark on this extraordinary journey together, where the stakes are high and the outcomes uncertain. A Presto!

Each participant will assume the role of a high-ranking mafia member, each with their own ambitions, loyalties, and secrets. The committee is tasked with reestablishing control, managing alliances, and securing the mafia’s future. Key decisions must be made about whether to escalate the conflict, seek peace with rivals, or forge new alliances with international criminal networks. As government forces close in and public opinion sways, every move could mean the difference between maintaining the empire or seeing it crumble.

I grew up as an expat kid--living in Australia, Scotland, Singapore, and the States--before ending up here in sunny California. Although I normally compete on the more GA side of things, I am a big believer that collegiate MUN is too repetitive from an ROP perspective and want to bring some of the "need for change" energy to the crisis cabinets. Jasmine and I have plenty of fun twists and turns planned out for our time together, so act like a true Italian gangster and just run with it. I can't wait to see everyone in February for UCBMUN XXIX!
