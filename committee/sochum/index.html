SOCHUM: Tackling the Digital Era

Position Paper due date TBD.

Background Guide coming soon.

Contact: sochum@ucbmun.org



Welcome to the Social, Humanitarian and Cultural Committee: Tackling the Digital Era. This committee takes place in the present day, and is meant to address the ways in which the digital world is impacting our political, social, and personal lives. Delegates are expected to use dynamic and collaborative solutions to tackle a plethora of issues, such as dis/misinformation, the growth of domestic extremism, the transitioning role of journalism, privacy concerns, international interference in elections, and more. The key tenant of the digital age is the amount of power it grants the individual, and how it bolsters connections between formerly disparate entities (civilians to leaders, fans to celebrities, companies to consumers). This committee is an opportunity to navigate this change and usher in a new era of international collaboration that is efficient, impactful, and ethical.

Hello!

My name is Avani Yadav (she/her), and I am a senior at Berkeley studying Applied Math and Economics with a minor in English. I am originally from Hershey, Pennsylvania, and have been competing on the college circuit for over 3 years. This is my third consecutive year chairing the big GA, and this year's topic is my favorite so far. My academic and personal interests revolve around the internet: I am a lab manager at the Human Rights Center at Berkeley Law, where I work with advocacy and journalism partners on projects at the intersection of tech and human rights; I spent my summer doing disinformation research for a think tank; I intend to eventually pursue tech law.

The digital world is my second home, which is why I know it is an almost entirely unregulated terrain. It has the power to fundamentally reshape our institutions, making this topic more vital than ever. The key to this committee is innovation – a modern problem requires modern solutions, and I am thrilled to be able to witness what you all bring to San Francisco, the beating heart of the tech world. See you all soon!

Warmly,
Avani Yadav
Chair, SOCHUM: Tackling the Digital Era
