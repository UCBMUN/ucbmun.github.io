adhoc-ga@ucbmun.org


The Ad–Hoc General Assembly of the Director–General is a premier committee of high–caliber debate at UCBMUN XXVII. The committee's topic itself will remain hidden and will be announced shortly before the conference begins. However, more details regarding committee structure and operation will be released soon.




VISHWAA SOFAT

Chair

Hola! Mi nombre es Vishwaa and I am ecstatic to be one of your Chairs for the Ad–hoc Committee of the Director–General. I am a junior from New York City, avec specialisation en science politique et science, technologie et societe. Having participated in Model UN throughout high school, MUN, especially GA committees, holds a special place in my heart. I served as one of UCBMUN's Head Delegates last year and am currently serving as the President. Alongside Model UN, I run my own civics initiative and write for the Daily Californian. I hope you take this opportunity to learn about new issues and work collectively with other delegates towards a solution.

Best,
VISHWAA SOFAT
Chair, Ad–Hoc General Assembly of the Director–General
