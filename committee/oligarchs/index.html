Russian Oligarchs

Background Guide coming soon.

Contact: oligarchs@ucbmun.org



By March 2014, the Russian state had made major geopolitical moves spanning their entire region. In Ukraine, Russian forces annex Crimea; in Syria, Russian mercenaries intervene in the Civil War; back home, the dissident Navalny is jailed, and Putin retains strong domestic power. Behind the machinations of the Russian government, a set of extremely wealthy individuals with a portfolio spanning the globe pull the strings. These are Russia's oligarchs: from media to weapons, from sports to drugs, these nobles control massive corporations that not only influence the future of Russia, but that of the world. Given these developments, Putin has "invited" you to a meeting of oligarchs to steer the Russian state to greater power.

Hey Delegates!

My name is Jet Situ and I'm very excited to be your Crisis Director for this committee! This crisis is extremely close to the present timeline, and many of you may remember the events taking place here. As a foreign extemporaneous speaker in high school, I covered these events extensively as they unfolded, and made some predictions – some of which didn't come to pass in reality, but may come true under your actions and goals. The background guide doesn't do nearly enough justice to the complexity of the topics we'll cover, and given the ongoing/recent nature of the conflicts covered, an arsenal of information online exists for additional research. Outside of UCBMUN, I'm part of an aerospace engineering club, and play BeatSaber on the college competitive circuit. In my free time, you'll find me at tea or ice cream places (I love sugar), hanging out with my friends, or taking walks around campus. When spring comes, I hope to see you all in commiteee, in person!
