About the Committee

Before now, the Lost City of Atlantis has been just that- lost. As nations above the water have made alliances, traded goods, and waged wars, Atlantis has been secretly growing its power as an underwater city-state. Now, in 2026, Atlantis wants to emerge from secrecy as a global superpower. As a council composed of members of Atlantis's government, you must decide how to do this. Will Atlantis choose to make peaceful contact with the rest of the world? Or will it leverage its hidden might to wage war? You must work together as a council to develop global alliances, communications strategies, and internal defenses to help Atlantis establish itself in the world. Atlantis's unique technologies and mythical ties to the water will be at your full disposal, but it's up to you to use these resources while balancing diplomacy with modern nations. The fate of Atlantis is up to you- will Atlantis emerge as a true superpower? Or will it sink back into the waves, lost to mythology?
