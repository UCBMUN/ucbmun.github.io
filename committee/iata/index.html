Up in the Air 1945: International Air Transport Association

Position Papers will be due before February 20, 2021. Submission instructions will be sent out one month prior to conference.

Background Guide will be released in January 2021.

Contact: iata@ucbmun.org



The year is 1945. With a world emerging out of its second global war in three decades, a little known trade association, the International Air Transport Association, is formed. It markets itself as a consortium of the world's largest airlines, helping to move the then–nascent commercial aviation industry forward and promote safety, efficiency and passenger satisfaction. As the leaders of world's foremost airlines, your job is to battle it out in the industry for dominance over the skies and control of the market. It's capitalism at its best and nastiest; but deep within, a darker secret lurks...


Manisha Sahoo

Hello!

I can't wait to see you in committee and I hope you're looking forward to it.

Good luck!

Winston Purnomo

Chair

Delegates, a warm welcome to the IATA committee! My name is Winston Purnomo and I will be your Chair in this year's UCBMUN. As a travel fanatic, I'm interested in seeing how the early members of IATA will handle a foundational moment in the early days of international air travel, and what kind of alliances, deals and power struggles we can see emerge out of committee. As for me, I'm a second–year CS major originally from Indonesia, with four years of experience in MUN. In addition, I'm the co–Head of Operations, Technology and Design for this year's conference, and hold a similar position for the Indonesian Student Association. I'm looking forward to meeting all of you at UCBMUN!
