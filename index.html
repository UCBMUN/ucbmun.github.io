UC Berkeley Model UN

March 5th to March 8th, 2026

To Our Esteemed Delegates from around the World,

Welcome to UCBMUN XXX! We are Audrey Watson and Cheyenne Jemison, and we are honored to serve as your Secretary-General and Director-General for the oldest premier collegiate Model UN conference on the West Coast. We are thrilled, on behalf of our wonderful Secretariat and amazing Staff, to welcome you back to the InterContinental San Francisco to participate in a weekend full of lively, intellectual, and enjoyable debate. We are delighted to see what creative ideas all of our delegates come up with this year!

Famous for its creativity, counterculture, and commitment to social justice, San Francisco is the perfect place to host one of the largest West Coast conferences on the Collegiate circuit. As the city that hosted the signing of the UN Charter in 1945, San Francisco holds a special place in the spirit of debate, and UCBMUN holds these ideals close to heart. At UCBMUN, we aim to create truly innovative committees and challenge delegates to reach their full potential in international debate. Whether going back in time to discuss European politics with our UNSC or into the future to debate military applications of emerging technologies with Disec. Our team of over 100 staffers, all with competitive experience on the circuit, is committed to fostering a weekend filled with substantive debate, inventive committee mechanics, and the opportunity to connect with your fellow delegates both inside and outside the committee.

At UCBMUN, the well-being of our delegates is our utmost priority. Please do not hesitate to reach out to us regarding anything from conference registration to the best places to eat in San Francisco! We are always available to address any issues that may arise. Once again, on behalf of us and our staff, we are beyond excited to welcome you back to the historic city of San Francisco. Join us at UCBMUN from March 5th to March 8th, 2026, for an unforgettable weekend. We can’t wait to meet you!

Best Regards,

Audrey Watson
