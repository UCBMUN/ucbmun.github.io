UCBMUN XXVII

UC Berkeley Model UN

March 2nd to March 5th, 2023

LETTER FROM THE SECRETARY-GENERAL

Dear Esteemed Delegates,

My name is Supriya Sharma, and I am thrilled to be the Secretary-General for the 27th iteration of UC Berkeley's premier collegiate Model United Nations Conference (UCBMUN XXVII). On behalf of all my Secretariat and Staff, it is my pleasure to invite you to San Francisco once again to debate, create policy, and meet other delegates from across the globe. Additionally, we are excited to announce that we will be hosting our conference this year at a new location, the Intercontinental San Francisco, a hotel in Union Square, in an area with so much to offer its visitors.

Located in Union Square, our conference will aim to channel San Francisco's tenant of ‘progress above all.’ Whether you are working towards building your drug trade and avoiding the attention of Federal agents in our Breaking Bad Committee, debating the fate of RuPaul's Drag Race, or discussing Ireland's status in the EEC in Irish Parliament, our committees will challenge and dare delegates to think beyond the basics. While you are here, we hope you also take the time to explore the city, more than even just a few blocks surrounding the hotel. At UCBMUN, however, we would like to stress that the support and well-being of our delegates is our top concern; if you ever have any issues, please do not hesitate to flag down any of our staffers.

Once again, it is my absolute honor to invite you to our conference in the city that birthed the historic UN charter hosted by the university home to the Free Speech Movement. Join us from March 2nd, 2023, to March 5th, 2023, for an iconic UCBMUN.

Best Regards,

Supriya Sharma
Secretary-General, UCBMUN XXVII
