UCBMUN XXIX

UC Berkeley Model UN | February 27th—March 2rd, 2025

LETTER FROM THE DIRECTORS

To our esteemed delegates from around the world,

Welcome to UCBMUN XXIX! Our names are Adinath Lane and Olivia Lapinsky, and we are incredibly excited to serve as your Secretary- General and Director-General for the oldest premier collegiate Model UN conference on the West Coast. On behalf of our Secretariat and Staff, it is our pleasure to welcome you back to the InterContinental San Francisco for another exciting weekend of thought-provoking and captivating debate. We cannot wait for you to see what we have in store this year!

Both San Francisco and Berkeley are famed for their creativity, counterculture, and commitment to justice. UCBMUN holds these ideals close to heart, creating truly innovative committees that challenge delegates to reach their full potential. Whether navigating the circuit’s first double-delegation GA Ad-Hoc or surviving the Upside Down in our Stranger Things committee, we are confident that your weekend will be both informative and exhilarating. Our team of over 100 staffers, all with competitive experience on the circuit, is committed to fostering a weekend filled with substantive debate, inventive committee mechanics, and the opportunity to connect with your fellow delegates both inside and outside committee.

At UCBMUN, the well-being of our delegates is our utmost priority. Please do not hesitate to reach out to us–we are always available to address any issues that may arise. Once again, on behalf of our staff, we are beyond excited to welcome you back to the historic city of San Francisco. Join us at UCBMUN from February 27 th to March 2nd, 2025 for an unforgettable weekend. We can’t wait to meet you!

Best Regards,


Adinath Lane
Secretary-General, UCBMUN XXIX
