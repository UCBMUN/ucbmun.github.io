Sponsor & Support UCBMUN XXVII

UCBMUN XXVII brings together passionate and challenge-oriented undergraduates from across the world. UCBMUN is one the most historic and prestigious student-run United Nations simulations in the United States, bringing together hundreds of delegates from across the world and UC Berkeley student staff for a weekend of problem-solving, negotiation, debate, and global engagement. Now in its 27th edition, UCBMUN provides delegates a forum in which to discuss the most urgent and pressing issues of today while also studying the past.

To learn more about our sponsorship packages and incentives, please see our sponsorship packet or contact sponsorships@ucbmun.org. We look forward to working with you!
