SPONSORSHIP


Each year, the generous contributions of our sponsors help us make UCBMUN the best it can be. Their support is instrumental in fostering a weekend of debate and learning for hundreds of college students with diverse interests and backgrounds. We offer a wide variety of sponsorship package options and benefits to ensure that our sponsors’ unique needs are met. Learn more about what makes UCBMUN a great sponsorship opportunity as well as our package offerings in the sponsorship packet linked at the top of the page.

If you are interested in sponsoring UCBMUN, please contact our Director of Business Relations, Jomari Encarnacion, at sponsorships@mun.berkeley.edu to get the process started.
