Submission of Registration & Payment

Upon submitting your registration form, our Director of Delegate Affairs will reach out to confirm your submission. After this is done, please expect an invoice for payment within two weeks of submitting your registration form. Please try to submit your payment within one month of receiving the invoice. If there are any questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to contact secretarygeneral@ucbmun.org.

Visa Letters

UCBMUN XXV is proud to welcome and support delegations from across the world. Thus, if your delegation is in need of Visa letters, please fill out the registration form, then reach out to the Director of Delegate Affairs at delaffairs@ucbmun.org as soon as possible.

Refund Policy

Delegation Withdrawal

If your whole delegation chooses not to attend UCBMUN XXV, the refund policy will be dictated by the following dates and delegations fees correlate to their total payment:
  • Full Refund - Before January 5, 2021
  • Half (50%) Refund - January 5, 2021 - February 10, 2021
  • No refunds will be given after February 10, 2021

    Delegate Withdrawal

    Delegations may add or drop delegates at any time. However, they must release claimed delegates before February 10 in order to receive a full refund on the delegate fee. All delegates dropped after February 10 are required to pay the delegate fee in full.

    Position Assignments

    Please note that delegations will only receive their committees and assignments after payment can be confirmed. If you have any extenuating circumstances, please reach out to secretarygeneral@ucbmun.org.
