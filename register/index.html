Submission of Registration & Payment

Upon submitting your registration form, our Chief of Staff, External will reach out to confirm your submission. After this is done, please expect an invoice for payment within two weeks of submitting your registration form. Please try to submit your payment within one month of receiving the invoice. If there are any questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to contact external@ucbmun.org.

Visa Letters

UCBMUN XXVI is proud to welcome and support delegations from across the world. However, UCBMUN is not responsible for any visa-related costs or refunds. The deadline to request a visa letter is January 15th. To request a visa letter, please reach out to external@ucbmun.org.

Delegate Fee Refund Policy

  • 100% Refund - prior to January 15th, 2022
  • 50% Refund - January 15th, 2022 - February 15th, 2022
  • Delegate fees are non-refundable after February 15th, 2022.

    Discounts
