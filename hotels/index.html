HOTEL



Intercontinental San Francisco

UCBMUN XXVII will take place at the Intercontinental San Francisco, located on Union Square of San Francisco. UCBMUN will be offering discounted rooms for the conference, and a booking link will be sent following registration for the conference. For any other questions related to booking hotel rooms for the conference, contact our Chief of Staff - External at external@ucbmun.org.

FLIGHTS

UCBMUN XXVII will be offering discounted flight rates for United and Delta airline flights. Information on how to book these flights will be sent in a confirmation email following registration.
