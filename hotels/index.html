HOTELS AND FLIGHTS














Intercontinental San Francisco

We are honored to host UCBMUN XXIX at the Intercontinental Hotel in San Francisco! Located near Union Square at 888 Howard Street and five minutes away from where the United Nations charter was signed, this world-class hotel is at the heart of the city. We’re excited to announce that UCBMUN registrants also obtain discounted rates at the Intercontinental Hotel for $189/night!



Flights

UCBMUN is also thrilled to present our partnerships with multiple airlines to provide delegates with flight discounts, including Delta, Southwest, and Alaska Airlines. For more information on either our hotel or flights, contact us at registration@mun.berkeley.edu!
