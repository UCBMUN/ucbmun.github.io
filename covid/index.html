COVID-19 Policies

As the public health situation continues to evolve, UCBMUN will remain flexible in our policies to combat the spread of COVID-19. Based on current guidelines, we plan to hold our conference fully in-person this spring. Our COVID-19 policy will be finalized two weeks before the conference and sent out to all registered schools. We plan to follow CDC, the state of California, the City of San Francisco, University of California, Berkeley, and Hilton Financial Districts guidelines in order to best preserve the health and safety of staffers and delegates alike. If you have any questions or concerns, please reach out to secretarygeneral@ucbmun.org.

External Policy

  • All delegates will be required to show proof of vaccination (must provide proof of full vaccination and be two weeks past the second dose) AND a negative COVID-19 test taken within 72 hours of the start of conference. Tests must be taken 2/28-3/2.
  • All delegates will be required to wear masks in public indoor spaces, including committee and other UCBMUN sponsored events, regardless of vaccination status. Masks must be worn over the mouth and nose and anyone refusing to comply with the mask mandate will be asked to leave.
  • Anyone displaying symptoms of COVID-19 will be asked to isolate and leave the conference, regardless of vaccination or testing status.
  • All delegates and faculty advisors will be asked to sign a liability waiver related to COVID-19, releasing UCBMUN and its staff from any fault if someone does test positive.
  • Delegates requesting refunds due to COVID-19 infection will be refunded in full.
  • COVID-19 policies from the Hilton hotel can be found at https://www.hilton.com/en/corporate/cleanstay/
  • Delegates may be subject to temperature checks. If delegates have a fever, they will be asked to isolate and not attend committee sessions.
  • Failure to comply with any and all UCBMUN COVID-19 restrictions will result in removal from the conference and disqualification from awards.

Internal Policy

  • All staffers will be required to show proof of vaccination (must provide proof of full vaccination and be two weeks past the second dose) AND a negative COVID-19 test taken within 72 hours of the start of conference. Tests must be taken 2/28-3/2.
  • You will be required to wear your mask at all times in indoor public spaces, including committee rooms, Jackson, and UCBMUN sponsored events.
  • Anyone displaying symptoms of COVID-19 will be asked to isolate and leave the conference, regardless of vaccination or testing status.
  • Failure to comply with any and all UCBMUN COVID-19 restrictions may result in removal from the conference and/or the club.
