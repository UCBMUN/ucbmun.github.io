COVID-19 Policies

UCBMUN XXVII is committed to keeping its delegates and staffers safe over the duration of conference. Thus, UCBMUN is continuing to examine current health guidelines and closely following the COVID-19 pandemic. We will be in compliance with COVID-19 policies of the CDC, the city of San Francisco, and UC Berkeley. Please feel free to email us at secretarygeneral@ucbmun.org or directorgeneral@ucbmun.org with any questions you may have!
